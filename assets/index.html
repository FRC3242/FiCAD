Welcome to FiCAD

A powerful tool designed by Team 3242 "Illumicats" for FRC Robotics electrical schematics.

About This Program

Team 3242, the "Illumicats" wanted an electrical CAD program to draw out our schematics for FRC Robotics quickly and efficiently. This is where FiCAD comes in.<

Features

  • Drag-and-drop electrical components
  • Real-time circuit simulation
  • Up-to-date electrical components
  • Printing to PDF
